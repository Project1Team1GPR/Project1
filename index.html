How to Use the Application:

Step 1: Please enter your name and weight and hit the "Save Info" button.


Step 2: Please select an activity from the drop down that you would like to perform in order to burn of the calories you consumed from your recipe of choice (you will select later).


Step 3: Please input a specific type of food or recipe you would like to browse recipes for. An example would be "chicken."


Step 4: After your personal information has been saved and you have selected an activity and input a type of food, please hite the "Submit Recipe" button.


Step 5: A list or recipes will load with information on how long it will take you to burn off those calories based on the activity you chose.


Step 6: To view the list of ingredients, click on the "Ingredients" button.


Step 7: The ingredients list will also include a link to the recipe page with step-by-step information on how to make the recipe.


Step 8: Enjoy!
